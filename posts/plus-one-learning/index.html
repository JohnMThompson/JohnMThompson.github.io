Plus or Minus One

Outline

Concepts

  • Easy to get stuck in lane
  • All Technologies expire
  • Learn +1/-1 step from where you are comfortable
  • Doesn’t have to be deep knowledge, conceptual knowledge works great
  • Can be:
    • Moving up/down a tech stack
    • Moving from low code to programming
    • GUI to terminal
    • Moving from tech to business domain
    • Moving from user of technology to deployer of technology
    • Doesn’t have to be moving to more technical areas
    • Closed to open source
  • “Stitching” together areas of knowledge/Learning at the borders
    • “See The Whole Board”

My Examples

  • I worked in BI and Data Visualization
    • Lots of Tableau and Alteryx - low code tools
    • Comfortable using GUI based tools and was able to accomplish goals
    • Started to think about career
      • What if I move to a company that doesn’t have x tool?
      • I wanted portable skills
    • SQL! Python!
  • I worked in Product Management
    • Wanted to better understand the work my engineering team was doing
    • Deployed some servers, learned Linux
  • I worked with closed source tools
    • For Personal Projects, I couldn’t afford these tools
    • Enter open source tools (Googled “Open Source Alteryx” or “Open Source Data Viz”)
    • KNIME, Metabase, Superset

How to Learn

  • Find ways to sit with someone at your workplace
    • Great if it’s available, not all workplace cultures will support
  • Try public competitions or challenges like Advent of Code, Makeover Monday, etc
    • Can be great if your blocker is finding content to create with
    • Can be overly vendor led and specific
  • Do a personal project that is NOT Hello World
    • Starting with Hello World is great to learn syntax and structure
    • Build a portfolio!
    • Find things in your life
      • hobbies
      • things that are frustrating
      • things you want to track over time

Curiosity, Exploration, and Being Bad

  • Having an openness to starting something new
  • Having a willingness to explore a new area
  • Being okay with making mistakes, maybe even needing to ditch and start over.
